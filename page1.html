B-Roll Challenge

The subject matter I chose was an intersection outside a shopping mall. The reason for choosing this was to show contrast. An intersection is extremely busy and very loud. Shooting at night shows how quiet the city gets and with the choice of music: Love Supreme - Lonely feelings, I feel that it emphasises the loneliness of the quiet night.
